About Shramik Shanti Campus

A legacy of academic excellence and community service in the heart of Lalitpur, Nepal since 1997.

Our Legacy

Shaping the Future of Technology, Law, and Intellect

Shramik Shanti Campus was founded with a visionary mandate: to cultivate high-performance academic learning environments that bridge the gap between theoretical concept loops and global marketplace execution targets. Nestled in the thriving center of Chyasal, Shankhamul, Lalitpur-9, the college stands as a premier reference venue for undergraduate studies.

Since its inception, our institution has maintained a steadfast commitment to high-end academic standards. As a proud constituent campus of Pokhara University, we follow a strictly monitored curriculum designed to empower the next generation of pioneers, innovators, and legal scholars.

Today, our campus proudly hosts over 1,200 students across three bachelor's programs — BCSIT, BALLB, and BAIDS — and continues to grow as a pillar of higher education in Lalitpur. By integrating modern hardware laboratory systems, an extensive research database registry, and mock trial courtrooms, we ensure our student cohorts are thoroughly engineered for competitive global workforce placement.

Affiliation

Affiliated with Pokhara University

Shramik Shanti Campus is proudly affiliated with Pokhara University (PU), one of Nepal's premier national universities established in 1997. All our degree programs — BCSIT, BALLB, and BAIDS — are recognized by Pokhara University and adhere to its rigorous academic standards and examination systems.
