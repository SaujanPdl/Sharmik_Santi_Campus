Our Story

A Campus Built on the Values of Hard Work & Peace

Shramik Shanti Campus — whose name translates to "Laborer's Peace" — was founded with the noble purpose of bringing quality higher education to the working community of Nepal. Nestled in Chyasal, Shankhamul, Lalitpur-9, the campus has grown from a modest institution into a respected centre of learning.

Since its establishment, Shramik Shanti Campus has maintained a steadfast commitment to academic excellence, producing graduates who serve Nepal's public and private sectors with distinction. Affiliated with Pokhara University, the campus follows a rigorous academic curriculum while nurturing the individual potential of every student.
